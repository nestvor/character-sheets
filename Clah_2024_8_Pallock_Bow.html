Features & Traits

Origin feats · class features · subclass · eldritch invocations

Tireless Reveler Origin Feat

When an ally you can see within 60 feet of yourself expends Heroic Inspiration, you can gain Heroic Inspiration if you lack it.

You can use this benefit 3 times (= Proficiency Bonus); all uses refresh on a Short or Long Rest.

Lucky Origin Feat

You have 3 Luck Points (= Proficiency Bonus), regained on a Long Rest.

Spend 1 when you make a d20 Test (attack, check, or save) to roll a second d20 and choose either die; or spend 1 to force an attacker to reroll an attack against you. One Luck Point per roll.

Fey Touched Feat · 4th level

Ability Score Increase: Charisma +1 (already included).

You learn Misty Step and one 1st-level spell of your choice from the Divination or Enchantment school — chosen spell: Command.

You can cast each of these spells once without a spell slot, and you regain that free casting on a Long Rest. You can also cast them using any spell slots you have. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for both.

Pact Magic Warlock 1

Charisma is your spellcasting ability for Warlock spells. Spell Save DC 15, Spell Attack +7.

You have 2 spell slots of 4th level, regained after a Short or Long Rest. All Warlock spells are cast at 4th level when using Pact Magic.

Magical Cunning Warlock 2

You can perform an intricate ritual that replenishes your pact's magic. As a Magic action, regain 1 expended Pact Magic spell slot (half your total, rounded up).

Once used, this refreshes on a Long Rest.

Celestial Spells Celestial · Warlock 3

The following spells are always prepared and don't count against spells known:

Cantrips: Light, Sacred Flame
1st: Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt
2nd: Lesser Restoration, Aid
3rd: Daylight, Revivify
4th: Guardian of Faith, Wall of Fire

Healing Light Celestial · Warlock 3

You have a pool of 8d6 healing dice (Warlock level + 1). As a Bonus Action, you or a creature you can see within 60 feet regains HP equal to the roll of up to 4 dice (= CHA mod) from the pool.

Pool resets on a Long Rest. Remember: Gift of the Ever-Living Ones makes healing dice rolled on you become maximum (6 each).

Pact of the Blade Invocation · Pact Boon

Bonus Action to bond a magic weapon you touch — your +1 shortbow or +1 quarterstaff. Attack & damage use Charisma, and you may choose its damage type (incl. Radiant). You're proficient with it and it serves as a spellcasting focus.

Thirsting Blade Invocation · req. Pact of the Blade

When you take the Attack action on your turn, you can attack twice with your bonded weapon instead of once.

Pact of the Chain · Lousy Pact Boon

Your familiar Lousy — a willful celestial spirit who appears as a cat — is bound to you. You learn Find Familiar and can cast it as a ritual.

Familiar attack via your turn. When you take the Attack action, you can forgo one of your own attacks so Lousy can make one attack of his own as a Reaction.

The spirit, not the form. Lousy is a celestial spirit. The Find Familiar spell summons him into a body — that body's stat block is what's listed on page 3. He typically manifests as the Imp form (cat-shaped, with celestial sparkle in his eyes), but on re-summoning Clah can ask the Star to send him in any Pact of the Chain form: Sphinx of Wonder for big set-pieces, Octopus underwater, regular cat for taverns. Personality and name persist across forms; only the body's stat block changes.

Eldritch Mind Invocation

You have Advantage on Constitution saving throws that you make to maintain Concentration.

Gift of the Ever-Living Ones Invocation · requires Pact of the Chain

Whenever you regain Hit Points while your familiar is within 100 feet of you, treat any dice rolled to determine the HP you regain as having rolled their maximum value.

Works with Healing Light, Cure Wounds, Prayer of Healing, potions, etc.

Spellcasting · Paladin Paladin 1

Charisma is your spellcasting ability. Spell Save DC 15, Spell Attack +7.

As a half-caster multiclass you have 2 × 1st-level slots (long rest). Prepared: Divine Favor, Bless (and Bane). Riding these keeps your Pact slots free.

Radiant Soul Celestial · Warlock 6

You have Resistance to Radiant damage.

Once per turn, when a spell you cast deals Radiant or Fire damage, add your Charisma modifier (+4) to one damage roll — rides Divine Favor on a bow hit and Sacred Flame.

Lessons of the First Ones → Magic Initiate (Druid) Invocation · grants an Origin feat

This invocation grants the Magic Initiate (Druid) feat with Charisma as its spellcasting ability:

Cantrips: Guidance, Elementalism
1st-level spell: Goodberry — cast it free once per Long Rest, or with any spell slot.

Berry battery. Magic Initiate lets you cast Goodberry with Pact slots — which recharge on a Short Rest. Spend spare slots before each rest to stockpile 10-HP-each berries (last 24 h, no concentration) for the whole party.

Lay on Hands Paladin 1

Pool of 5 HP (5 × Paladin level), refreshes on a Long Rest. As a Bonus Action, touch a creature to restore HP from the pool.

Or spend 5 from the pool to end one Poisoned condition.

Weapon Mastery Paladin 1

You use the mastery property of 2 weapon kinds (swap on a Long Rest):

Vex (shortbow) — on a hit, you have Advantage on your next attack against that target.
Topple (quarterstaff) — on a hit, the target makes a CON save (DC 15) or has the Prone condition.

Human Traits Species

Resourceful. You gain Heroic Inspiration whenever you finish a Long Rest.

Skillful. Proficiency in one extra skill (Deception).

Versatile. An Origin feat from your species + one from your Urchin background → Tireless Reveler & Lucky.

Glimmering Orb requires attunement

While attuned and holding the orb:

  • Spells you cast ignore resistance to radiant and necrotic damage.
  • 1 / Short Rest: when a creature would make a saving throw against a spell you cast, impose disadvantage on every creature making that save — not just one target. AoE spells get the full effect.

Radiant sources that bypass resistance: Divine Favor (on bow/staff hits), Sacred Flame, Guiding Bolt, Sickening Radiance, Radiant Soul. Note: Lousy's Sting is not affected — Orb applies only to spells Clah casts.

Ring of Spell Storing ring · rare · requires attunement

Stores spells of level 1–3 cast into it, holding them until you use them. Capacity: 3 spell levels total (reduced variant — standard is 5).

Any creature can cast a spell of level 1–3 into the ring by touching it as they cast. When you cast from the ring, the spell uses the slot level, save DC, attack bonus, and spellcasting ability of the original caster — but otherwise behaves as if you cast it. No slot expended by you.

Great emergency options to store: Counterspell, Shield, or Absorb Elements (loaded by a caster ally). Also stash one of your own spells for later — e.g., prime Misty Step or Dimension Door so you can escape without spending your action on the key turn.

Mantle of Spell Resistance cloak · rare · requires attunement

You have advantage on saving throws against spells while wearing this cloak.

Applies only to saves vs. spells — not against breath weapons, innate monster abilities, or traps (unless the DM rules a specific effect is a spell). Pairs beautifully with Eldritch Mind, which already gives advantage on CON concentration saves.

+1 Half-Plate armor · rare

You have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. Base half-plate AC 15 + DEX (max 2) + 1 shield + 1 magic = 20 (with shield equipped).

Disadvantage on Stealth checks from armor (not applied here — worth checking with DM if Stealth matters).

+1 Quarterstaff · +1 Shortbow weapon · uncommon

You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with these weapons. Attacks count as magical.

Using Your Familiar

Attack via Pact of the Chain. On your turn, when you take the Attack action, forgo one of your own attacks; Lousy can then use his Reaction to Sting (+5, 1d6+3 piercing + 2d6 poison auto). Situational — your two bow attacks are usually stronger.
Share Senses + Devil's Sight. Bonus Action to see through Lousy. Combined with his Devil's Sight + 120 ft darkvision, Clah can see through magical darkness — including from his own Hunger of Hadar.
Permanent Invisible Scout. Send Lousy invisible into any area; Share Senses to perceive what he sees. Drop invisibility only to deliver a Sting or Help.
Invisible + Help action. Lousy stays invisible while taking the Help action — granting advantage on one bow attack per round without exposing himself.
Keep within 100 ft — telepathy (Find Familiar) + max heal dice on yourself (Gift of Ever-Living Ones).
The spirit, not the form. Lousy is a celestial spirit — body type for spell-interaction purposes (Banishment, Detect Evil and Good, etc.) is the form's type, but the spirit himself is celestial. On re-summoning, Clah can request other Pact of the Chain forms: Sphinx of Wonder for Burst of Ingenuity utility, Sprite for Heart Sight social use, Pseudodragon for blindsight 10 ft, or any standard Find Familiar form (octopus underwater, owl at night, regular cat in town). Personality and name persist; only the body's stat block changes.
On the name. "Lousy" because most of the time he acts like a regular cat — willful, napping, ignoring commands. A familiar that ignores you is, well, a lousy familiar. Clah loves him anyway.

Actions

Bonus Action

Fey Step (Tricksy). The spirit teleports up to 30 ft to a space it can see, then fills a 10-ft cube within 5 ft of itself with magical Darkness, lasting until the end of its next turn.
DM ruling: Tricksy darkness blocks darkvision (Darkness-spell-equivalent obscurement). Lousy's Devil's Sight pierces it; party tremorsense pierces it; standard darkvision does not.

Tactical Use

Skirmisher pattern. Fey moves to engage an enemy → Multiattack → Fey Step back to safety next to Clah, dropping the cube on that safe location. Avoids exposing the fey to retaliation and OAs.
Cube on Clah. You're inside heavy obscurement → Invisible condition. Enemies outside attacking you have disadvantage; you have advantage on attacks against them (unseen attacker rule). Lousy's Devil's Sight + Share Senses → see through cube to target.
Cube as wall (line-of-sight block). Position the cube between Clah and the enemies. Heavy obscurement is opaque per 2024 RAW — blocks line of sight through it. Enemies can't see you; you see them via Lousy's Devil's Sight + Share Senses → advantage on EB (unseen attacker), and enemies attacking back have disadvantage + must guess your square. Fey repositions the cube each round to chase flanking attempts.
Fey's vision. Fey only has standard darkvision — blinded inside its own cube. Cube ends at end of fey's next turn, so the fey acts freely on its turn before refreshing the cube. To attack from inside: place cube at edge of fey's space (10-ft cube within 5 ft → adjacent placement, not overlapping fey).
Tremorsense ally synergy. Party member with tremorsense can stand inside the cube alongside Clah — both concealed from enemies outside, both sensing freely inside.
Share Senses (2024). Bonus Action to perceive through Lousy. 2024 change: you no longer go blind/deaf to your own senses while sharing — you get Lousy's Devil's Sight in addition to your normal vision. No downside to using it every turn.
Verbal commands free. Direct the fey with speech — no action cost. If silenced or out of earshot, fey defaults to Dodge.
Initiative timing. Fey shares Clah's initiative, acts immediately after. Round 1 of casting: cube isn't up until end of fey's turn (setup tax). Round 2+: cube refreshes each round seamlessly.
Material focus: a gilded flower worth 300+ gp. Reusable — buy once, keep it safe.

Suggest a course of activity — 1 or 2 sentences — to a creature you can see that can hear and understand you. Target makes a Wisdom save (DC 15, CHA); on fail, it pursues the course for the duration or until completion.

Must sound reasonable. Activity can last the full duration. If completed earlier, spell ends. Directly harmful suggestions (attack, self-harm) automatically fail. Ends early if target takes damage.

Summon a Fey Spirit in unoccupied space within range. Acts on your initiative, just after your turn. Obeys verbal commands (free). Choose mood at cast — Clah's pick:

Tricksy Mood: Fey Step (Bonus Action) — teleport up to 30 ft, then fill a 10-ft cube within 5 ft with magical Darkness until end of next turn. DM ruled: this darkness blocks darkvision (like the Darkness spell).

★ The Combo: Tricksy fey teleports adjacent to Clah, drops the cube on him each round. Clah inside = Invisible condition; attackers outside have disadvantage. Lousy's Devil's Sight + Share Senses → advantage on every bow shot. Renewable every round.

Caveat: The fey only has standard darkvision — it's blinded inside its own cube. After Fey Step it should step outside the cube on its next turn (or attack from outside the cube), or the fey takes attack penalties too.

At higher levels: spell uses slot level for the Fey's stat block (HP, AC, attack, save DC). At Pact 3rd → CR 3 fey: AC 15, HP ~33, Multiattack ~13 dmg/turn.

A 60-foot-radius sphere of light springs from a point you choose within range. The sphere is Bright Light and sheds Dim Light for an additional 60 feet.

If you choose a point on an object you are holding or one that isn't being worn or carried, the light shines from that object and moves with it. Completely covering the affected object with something opaque blocks the light.
