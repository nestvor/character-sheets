Tireless Reveler Origin Feat

When an ally you can see within 60 feet of yourself expends Heroic Inspiration, you can gain Heroic Inspiration if you lack it.

You can use this benefit 3 times (= Proficiency Bonus); all uses refresh on a Short or Long Rest.

Spellfire Spark Origin Feat

Magic Absorption. Once per turn, when you take damage from a spell or magical effect, reduce the total damage taken by 1d4. Can't be used while Incapacitated.

Spellfire Flame. You know the Sacred Flame cantrip with Charisma as its spellcasting ability. You can cast it as a Bonus Action up to 3 times (= Proficiency Bonus); uses refresh on a Long Rest.

Fey Touched Feat · 4th level

Ability Score Increase: Charisma +1 (already included).

You learn Misty Step and one 1st-level spell of your choice from the Divination or Enchantment school — chosen spell: Command.

You can cast each of these spells once without a spell slot, and you regain that free casting on a Long Rest. You can also cast them using any spell slots you have. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for both.

Pact Magic Warlock 1

Charisma is your spellcasting ability for Warlock spells. Spell Save DC 15, Spell Attack +7.

You have 2 spell slots of 3rd level, which are regained after a Short or Long Rest. All Warlock spells are cast at 3rd level when using Pact Magic.

Magical Cunning Warlock 2

You can perform an intricate ritual that replenishes your pact's magic. As a Magic action, regain 1 expended Pact Magic spell slot (half your total, rounded up).

Once used, this refreshes on a Long Rest.

Celestial Spells Celestial · Warlock 3

The following spells are always prepared and don't count against spells known:

Cantrips: Light, Sacred Flame
1st: Cure Wounds, Guiding Bolt
2nd: Lesser Restoration, Aid
3rd: Daylight, Revivify

Healing Light Celestial · Warlock 3

You have a pool of 6d6 healing dice (Warlock level + 1). As a Bonus Action, you or a creature you can see within 60 feet regains HP equal to the roll of up to 4 dice (= CHA mod) from the pool.

Pool resets on a Long Rest. Remember: Gift of the Ever-Living Ones makes healing dice rolled on you become maximum (6 each).

Agonizing Blast Invocation · EB

When you cast Eldritch Blast, add your Charisma modifier (+4) to the damage dealt to the target on a hit. Applied to each beam.

Repelling Blast Invocation · EB

When you hit a Large or smaller creature with Eldritch Blast, you can push the creature 10 feet straight away from you (once per turn per beam — up to 20 ft total with both beams hitting the same target).

Pact of the Chain · Lousy the Sphinx Pact Boon

Your familiar Lousy — a Sphinx of Wonder — is bound to you. You learn Find Familiar and can cast it as a ritual. See full statblock on page 3.

Familiar attack via your turn. When you take the Attack action, you can forgo one of your own attacks so Lousy can make one attack of his own as a Reaction.

Rend is a solid attack (+5, avg 12 damage incl. radiant), but Clah casting Eldritch Blast as his action is almost always a better use of the turn. Lousy's real value is intelligence: flying scout, radiant-resistant tank, Burst of Ingenuity party assists, and eyes for Invoke Duplicity ranged casting.

Eldritch Mind Invocation

You have Advantage on Constitution saving throws that you make to maintain Concentration.

Gift of the Ever-Living Ones Invocation · requires Pact of the Chain

Whenever you regain Hit Points while your familiar is within 100 feet of you, treat any dice rolled to determine the HP you regain as having rolled their maximum value.

Works with Healing Light, Cure Wounds, Prayer of Healing, potions, etc.

Spellcasting · Cleric Cleric 1

Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for Cleric spells. Spell Save DC 12, Spell Attack +4.

Slots: 4 × 1st, 2 × 2nd. All Cleric spells on the class list are prepared by default (plus Trickery domain).

Thaumaturge (Divine Order) Cleric 1

You gain one Wizard cantrip of your choice (already counted — Thaumaturgy chosen).

You also add your Wisdom modifier (+1) to any Intelligence (Arcana) and Intelligence (Religion) check you make.

Channel Divinity Cleric 2

2 uses per Short or Long Rest. Two options available:

Divine Spark. Magic action. Point at target within 60 ft and roll 2d8 + WIS. Choose radiant or necrotic damage (CON save halves) OR heal for that amount.

Invoke Duplicity — see Trickery domain.

Invoke Duplicity Trickery · Cleric 3

Bonus Action (uses Channel Divinity). Create an illusory duplicate of yourself in an unoccupied space within 30 feet for 1 minute.

As a Bonus Action on later turns, move the duplicate up to 30 ft (max 120 ft from you). You have advantage on attack rolls against a creature within 5 ft of the duplicate. You can cast spells as though from the duplicate's space.

Blessing of the Trickster Trickery · Cleric 3

Magic action. Touch a willing creature other than yourself to give them advantage on Stealth checks for 1 hour or until you use this again.

Use 3 times per Long Rest (= Proficiency Bonus).

Trickery Domain Spells Trickery · Cleric 3

Always prepared, don't count against spells prepared:

1st: Charm Person, Disguise Self
2nd: Invisibility, Pass Without Trace

Magic Items
Sentinel Shield shield · uncommon · no attunement

While holding this shield, you have advantage on Initiative rolls and Wisdom (Perception) checks.

AC +2 included in your 20 AC. Passive Perception 11, but effectively ~13 due to the advantage.

Glimmering Orb requires attunement

While attuned and holding the orb:

  • Spells you cast ignore resistance to radiant and necrotic damage.
  • 1 / Short Rest: when a creature would make a saving throw against a spell you cast, impose disadvantage on every creature making that save — not just one target. AoE spells get the full effect.

Radiant spells that bypass resistance: Sacred Flame, Guiding Bolt, Word of Radiance, True Strike's bonus die. Note: Lousy's Rend is not affected — Orb applies only to spells Clah casts.

Ring of Spell Storing ring · rare · requires attunement

Stores spells of level 1–3 cast into it, holding them until you use them. Capacity: 3 spell levels total (reduced variant — standard is 5).

Any creature can cast a spell of level 1–3 into the ring by touching it as they cast. When you cast from the ring, the spell uses the slot level, save DC, attack bonus, and spellcasting ability of the original caster — but otherwise behaves as if you cast it. No slot expended by you.

Great emergency options: Counterspell, Shield, Absorb Elements, Silvery Barbs (if party wizard can cast), Healing Word from a cleric friend. Also stores one of your own spells for later — e.g., pre-cast Misty Step via the ring to save your BA on a key turn.

Mantle of Spell Resistance cloak · rare · requires attunement

You have advantage on saving throws against spells while wearing this cloak.

Applies only to saves vs. spells — not against breath weapons, innate monster abilities, or traps (unless the DM rules a specific effect is a spell). Pairs beautifully with Eldritch Mind, which already gives advantage on CON concentration saves.

+1 Half-Plate armor · rare

You have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. Base half-plate AC 15 + DEX (max 2) + 1 shield + 1 magic = 20 (with shield equipped).

Disadvantage on Stealth checks from armor (not applied here — worth checking with DM if Stealth matters).

+1 Quarterstaff · +1 Shortbow weapon · uncommon

You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with these weapons. Attacks count as magical.

Using Your Familiar

Attack via Pact of the Chain. On your turn, when you take the Attack action, forgo one of your own attacks; Lousy can then use his Reaction to Rend (+5, 1d4+3 slashing + 2d6 radiant — a solid hit). Situational, since casting Eldritch Blast is usually the stronger action.
Share Senses (your BA) — see/hear through Lousy until start of next turn; you are Blind/Deaf to your own senses. Darkvision 60 only — no blindsight.
Help action — Lousy can take Help on his own turn. Combined with Invisibility cast on him, he grants advantage on one Eldritch Blast beam per round safely.
Radiant damage type. Rend's 2d6 radiant is a great type against undead and fiends. Note: Glimmering Orb only bypasses resistance for spells Clah casts — Lousy's attacks are unaffected by the Orb.
Keep within 100 ft — telepathy (Find Familiar) + max heal dice on yourself (Gift of Ever-Living Ones).
On the name. "Lousy" because most of the time he acts like a regular cat — willful, napping, ignoring commands. A familiar that ignores you is, well, a lousy familiar. Clah loves him anyway.
